Chapter 11 Monitoring and evaluation of adaptation actions and process

The guiding framework for monitoring and evaluation is:

    1. Monitoring and evaluation system (mapped to goals and objectives, and targets where applicable; 5 types of metrics: inputs, process, output, outcome, impact). Indicators for each programme/project to be identified to track outcomes.
    1. Reporting on progress on NAPs under the UNFCCC (through National Communications/Adaptation Communications)
    1. Link to the NDC
    1. Reporting and outreach at the national level
    1. Use of the PEG M&E tool9 to monitor NAP process.
